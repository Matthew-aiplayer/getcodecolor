Frequently Asked Questions

Will my uploaded images be sent to a server?
No. All image processing in Get Color Code is done locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server. We take your privacy seriously, which is why we chose to process everything on the client side.
What image formats are supported?
JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and SVG are supported. You can also paste images directly from your clipboard using Ctrl+V or Cmd+V.
Can I see HEX, RGB, and HSL formats at the same time?
Yes. After clicking on an image, the sidebar displays HEX (e.g. #FF6B6B), RGB (e.g. rgb(255, 107, 107)), and HSL (e.g. hsl(0, 100%, 71%)) formats simultaneously. Click any format to copy it to your clipboard with one click.
Can I save multiple picked colors?
Yes. Each time you click on the image, the color is automatically saved to the color history panel on the right. You can click on any historical color to review it, or clear all records with one click.
Is this tool really completely free?
Yes, Get Color Code is completely free to use. No registration required, no hidden fees. We want to provide a simple, useful tool for designers, developers, and anyone who needs to pick colors from images.
